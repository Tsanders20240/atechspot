Our mission

Technology should create confidence, access, and opportunity.

A+ Techucation LLC was created to make technology more understandable, useful, and human.

Technology Within Us

A+ Techucation is built on the belief that people should not be made to feel ashamed, rushed, or excluded because technology is unfamiliar.

The mission is inspired by family, resilience, lifelong learning, and the people who keep moving forward through challenges.

  • Individuals with disabilities
  • Creators and entrepreneurs
  • Churches and nonprofits
  • Small businesses
