Meet the learning approach

Each letter becomes a starting point. Adults can ask what the word means, where the learner has seen it, and how technology can be used safely and creatively.

Technology becomes less intimidating when learners can connect it to words, pictures, music, and everyday experiences.

For parents and educators

  • Use one letter at a time
  • Connect words to real devices
  • Discuss safe and responsible use
  • Let learners draw and explain ideas
  • Repeat with video and music
