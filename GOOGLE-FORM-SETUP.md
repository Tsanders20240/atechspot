# Google Form Intake Setup

The website currently has a complete universal intake form at:

- https://www.atechspot.com/intake

It sends through the site's existing email-form system.

A Google Form must be created inside your own Google account. After creating it:

1. Go to Google Forms.
2. Create a blank form named:
   `A+ Techucation Universal Service Intake`
3. Add these fields:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Business or Organization
   - Service Requested
   - Preferred Meeting
   - Preferred Timeframe
   - Device / Website / Platform
   - Budget Range
   - Problem or Goal
   - Desired Outcome
   - Accessibility or Communication Needs
   - Acknowledgment
4. In Google Forms, click `Send`.
5. Choose the link icon and copy the public form URL.
6. Replace this placeholder in the website:
   `REPLACE_WITH_GOOGLE_FORM_URL`
7. Use the same Google Form link from every service page.

Recommended Zoom Scheduler:

https://scheduler.zoom.us/a-plus-techucation/30-mins-with-a-plus

Important:
- Do not collect passwords, Social Security numbers, full account numbers,
  payment-card data, or highly sensitive records in Google Forms.
- Turn on email notifications for new form responses in Google Forms.
